Overview:
1-2 players
solve multiplication problems to advance critter
first one to cross the finish line wins

pre-game:
- choose # of players
- choose background
- choose critter
- choose difficulty

game:
(2-player) Each player gets a randomly generated problem and multiple-choice answers.
When player chooses the correct answer using keyboard, the critter advances.
Sections:
- math problems
- race track
